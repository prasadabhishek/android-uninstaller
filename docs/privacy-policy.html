Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Data Collection

Bulk Uninstaller does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Permissions Used

  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES — Used to list installed apps on your device for uninstallation.
  • PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS — Optional. Used only to sort apps by last used date. This data stays on your device and is never shared.
  • BIND_ACCESSIBILITY_SERVICE — Optional. Used only to automatically confirm uninstall dialogs. This service does not send any data anywhere.

Data Storage

All preferences and settings are stored locally on your device only. No cloud sync, no accounts required.

Third Parties

This app contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

Changes

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
